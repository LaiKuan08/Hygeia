The Mission
Precision fermentation has the potential to redefine how the world produces food, ingredients, and wellness compounds.

The industry's biggest barrier isn't the science.

It's commercial infrastructure that doesn't exist yet.

Hygeia and Avante exist to build it. Starting with ASEAN. Scaling globally.
The Problem

The Science Works.
The Infrastructure Doesn't.

Precision fermentation companies across ASEAN are solving the biology. What stops them from reaching market is everything that comes after the lab — and none of it is built for this category yet.

Regulatory Pathways Are Fragmented and Company-Specific

Every fermentation company navigates Novel Food approvals from scratch. No consolidated intelligence, no shared frameworks, no regional playbook. Each approval costs years and capital that most startups don't have.

Formulation Expertise for This Category Doesn't Exist at Scale

Generic formulation platforms have no precision fermentation depth and no ASEAN regulatory context. The output is a document. What brands need is a commercialisation decision — those are not the same thing.

No Market Intelligence Layer Exists for the Category

No one tracks which fermentation ingredients are gaining traction, which applications are growing, or which buyers are actually paying — across ASEAN, in consolidated form, for this category specifically.

Hardware Infrastructure Is Priced for Pharma, Not Food

Enterprise bioprocess systems are built for biopharma margins. Emerging fermentation ingredient companies cannot absorb the CAPEX. Scale-up becomes the bottleneck even when the science is fully proven.
